### Boruvka's Algorithm

Vertex u:     

    p (pointer to the other endpoint of the shortest edge out of u) 
    Initially P(u) = u

Edge (u, v):  

    Next, l, v1, v2

        #

     (v, u)

#### Edge pardo:

    tnext = next;
    min = l;

    repeat:
      min = min[tnext.min, min];
      tnext = tnext.tnext;
    until:
      tnext = nil;
